About the site design

I decided to go for a very basic page design. I decided to go tih a textured background from subtlepatterns to have a little bit of texture on my page without having to worry about image copyrights. I decided to use boxes to house all of my text to help my text stand out against the background. I used a very simple color scheme which is very loosely analagous. Different colors are supposed to highlight different types of content.
